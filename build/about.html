What We Do

Warlox specializes in Open Source Intelligence (OSINT), web application penetration testing, and secure application development. Based in the Inland Northwest, USA with operations in Budapest, Hungary, we serve clients across the United States, Europe, and the Middle East.

Our OSINT capabilities include skiptracing, court paper delivery coordination, business due diligence investigations, and comprehensive digital footprint analysis.

We provide hands-on web application security testing with specialized expertise in .NET Core and PHP applications, combined with secure development consulting to help teams build defensible systems from the ground up.

Beyond technical testing, we offer adversarial thinking and red team counseling—helping businesses understand attacker methodologies and strengthen their security posture through strategic guidance.

All of our employees are US citizens and we maintain the highest ethical standards in every engagement.
